Aespa is back with a bop!


“Next Level” will be Aespa’s third single. Their latest, “Forever,” was released in February, while their debut single “Black Mamba” came out in November 2020.
Aespa is made up of Winter, Karina, Giselle, and Ningning. They are the newest girl group launched by SM Entertainment, the same label behind some of K-pop’s biggest acts such as Super Junior, Girls Generation, EXO, NCT, and Red Velvet.



After listening to this you'll be jamming "Bambi Bambi"


EXO’s Baekhyun is back with his new mini album “Bambi”!
The album contains six R&B tracks that deal with the topic of love. The title track, also named “Bambi,” is composed by DEEZ, YUNSU, SAAY, and Adrian McKinnon. Written by DEEZ and SAAY, the lyrics portray a mature love story using expressions used in fairytales. Watch Baekhyun’s “Bambi” music video above!



Hot Sauce keep it dip that


NCT DREAM delivers their first full-length album through 맛 (Hot Sauce), a daring distribution of varying genres in which among different concepts, the group mostly focuses on the storytelling of their brotherhood and unity that derives from their team spirit.
Ahead of its release, 맛 (Hot Sauce) reached a new milestone for NCT DREAM and its representative SM Entertainment, becoming the company’s most pre-ordered album of all time, with over 1.71 million accumulated stock pre-orders. This showcased a growth of 243% in sells since the group’s previous release, their fourth mini-album RELOAD.
